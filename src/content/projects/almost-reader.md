## What it is

Almost Reader is the project where I am exploring how reading experiences can feel atmospheric, adaptive, and emotionally tuned without becoming noisy or over-designed.

## What I am solving

Most reading apps are technically functional but emotionally flat. I want to build interfaces that respect focus, carry mood, and feel alive without distracting from the text.

## What makes it interesting

- Context-aware presentation instead of one static mode
- A strong relationship between content structure and interface
- Room for sound, ambience, and pacing as product features

## Current direction

This project sits at the intersection of product thinking, creative tooling, and experience engineering, and it informs how the writing engine on this site should evolve too.
