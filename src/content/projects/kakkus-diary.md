## Why this exists

I wanted a site that does not force me to choose between a polished portfolio and a messy idea space. The portfolio should show the best work, and the writing system should expose how the work is formed.

## Core ideas

- Obsidian stays the source of truth for writing
- Folder structure should shape the public knowledge architecture
- Metadata, tags, and wikilinks should become real navigation primitives
- The visual language should separate portfolio storytelling from writing density

## Current redesign

The current upgrade focuses on turning the homepage into a stronger portfolio, making writing a proper subsite, and supporting richer Obsidian syntax such as wikilinks, Mermaid, and DiceRoll.
