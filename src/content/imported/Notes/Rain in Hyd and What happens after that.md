# Rain in Hyd and What happens after that

## Summary
The rain itself is nice honestly but what happens after sweeps the ground from your feet (not sure if that is a thing :D)

---

## So What happens

Rains in hyderabad is a phenomenon which happens more often than you might think, cause people dont really assosiate hyderabad with rains, RIGHT. But youd be surprised man cause it rains after almost every season change it feels like.

So What happens is that the clouds roll in and the wind makes every window and door in your home wish they were not made of that cheap material that the contractor used to save money,
the wind howls like a pack of wolfs on a starry full moon night. it gets super windy (which i really like, id live in a wind tunnel if the logistics worked ;X). 

All of this is wonderfull but there is a catch

## What happens after the wind dies down and the Roads no longer reminds you of Venice.

Every dirt and grime of Hyderabad which was hiding from you, like the dust under the rugs which you sneaked hoping no one notices comes out rushing with the gushing waters but like a unwanted guest that you wish would leave as soon as possiable, they just stay there outliving their welcome, The roads get filled with all sorts of debris, all the pachy hacky repair job done till that time sheds the makeup done by the authorities and spread it all across the road making sure no one ignores them, 

The pachy repair job and the random piles of dirt and dust after the rain gets so evenly spread across the road that it becomes a round of takeshis castle and you on your 2 wheeler just get 3 objectives

- Avoid the stupids on the road
- Try not to fall
- Reach the destination alive

funny thing is the dirt is more like a gravel so even while walking you just slip around because its always the perfect amount not not be noticeable but enough to cause problems.
